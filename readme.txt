== Description ==
**Supported WooCommerce versions: 1.1.x – 1.6.x, 2.x, 3.x**
**Supported Pinnacle versions: 3.5.x - 3.8.x**

Cart2Cart: Pinnacle Cart to WooCommerce Migration plugin is an exceptional chance to move  from one platform to another swimmingly and accurately. Since this migration plugin is fully automated, it doesn’t require any programming skills or expertise. Everything you have to do is to establish a connection between the stores, choose entities to move and let Cart2Cart do everything for you. The following plugin is absolutely free to download, though migration itself is paid.

**The plugin offers 2 migration options**

1. Free Demo migration — lets you perform migration of products, orders, customers and categories in limited quantity (up to 10 entities each) for test purposes.
1. Full Migration — lets you perform UNLIMITED migration of products, orders, customers, categories, variants, attributes and other related entities. Full Migration price starts at $69 and may vary depending on the number of transferable entities and additional options chosen.

= Features =

* **Free Demo Migration.** Test the functionality of this plugin by performing demo migration and transferring up to 10 entities at no cost.
* **Secure and Reliable.** Your data is safe thanks to dedicated Amazon server used to perform the migration.
* **Uptime Guaranteed.** Your current Pinnacle Cart will be online during the transferring process, excluding the possibility of revenue decline because of migration.
* **Professional Support.** Qualified Support Team is always ready to consult you concerning any problem you’ve encountered.

**Transfer the following data from Pinnacle Cart to WooCommerce:**

* Products, product images, product attributes;
* Categories, category images;
* Customers, customer shipping address, customer billing address;
* Orders, order statuses, order products;
* Manufacturers, taxes;

**Please note:** Cart2Cart migrates data only, so your Pinnacle Cart design will not be transferred.

In order to perform an accurate transfer from Pinnacle Cart to WooCommerce, you need to follow a few simple steps. Firstly, you have to download the migration plugin. Afterwards, install it and establish the connection between your stores. Next, you’ll be automatically redirected to Cart2Cart website in order to continue migration. To test the plugin’s functionality, choose free Demo Migration option, that will move up to 10 entities to WooCommerce in terms of minutes. Finally, select Insurance plan (it’s not necessary, but recommended to save your money in case of remigration) and launch Full Migration to start automated data transfer.

**Pre-Migration Steps**

1. Install WooCommerce store. Ensure it can be reached via web
1. Check for FTP details for both of your shopping carts. They are required to perform migration.

== Installation ==

1. Download plugin.zip file and extract it to your hard drive
1. Upload unzipped file to wp-content/plugin directory
1. Activate “Cart2Cart Pinnacle Cart to WooCommerce Migration” in plugins section of your admin panel
1. Insert FTP details to upload the Connection Bridge files for Pinnacle Cart
1. Connection Bridge files for WooCommerce are will be uploaded automatically
1. Click Start Migration button and follow the Migration Wizard to perform accurate and swift migration

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

=Will my SEO be migrated? =
Unfortunately, SEO data is not moved during Pinnacle Cart migration. Therefore, be ready that store’s URLs and structure will be modified.
= Will you move customers credit card information? =
No, clients credit card info is not going to be migrated, but shipping and billing addresses will be transferred appropriately.
= Can my new hosting cause migration interruption? =
In case your store has a big number of entities, please make sure your hosting plan has enough space to accommodate all the data. Otherwise, Cart2Cart won’t be able to transfer your entities and migration will be interrupted.
